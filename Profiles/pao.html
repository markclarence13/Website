Description/info about yourself:

Leader of this group 1, organized, and friendly. He co-designed this website with his groupmates. Co-developer of OpenUtau, a free and open-source singing synthesizer program for standard and AI voicebanks. Paolo is an aromantic guy who's love to draw, code, and make covers with either singing or using singing synthesizers. He develop a recording list for ARPAsing, a proof of concept english recording method for a synthing synthesizer software, developed my Kanru Hua. video demo of the singing synthesizer, developed by Cadlaxa, aka Paolo Cadag. He also edits fan edits on his editing instagram during Paolo's bored pandemic phase. In addition to his technical and artistic endeavors, Paolo has made notable contributions to the development of ARPAsing, a pioneering English recording method for singing synthesizer software, originally developed by Kanru Hua. His work demonstrates a keen interest in advancing the capabilities of singing synthesizers, further evidenced by his development of a video demo for a singing synthesizer and a developed a custom english ARPAbet phonemizer, a project that highlights his innovative approach to music technology. His dedication to his passions—spanning from software development to digital art—underscores Paolo's unique blend of technical skill and creative flair.
