About Him

Mark Clarence B. Viernes

In 2008, he relocated to Imus City, Cavite from Makati, the vibrant city he grew up in. He embarked on a journey into the realm of fandom in 2017 when Taylor Swift's "Reputation" album left an indelible mark on his musical tastes. The artistry and storytelling prowess of Swift ignited his passion for her work, transforming him into a dedicated fan. Beyond the realm of music, his interests extend to the captivating world of period dramas. Whether immersing himself in the societal intricacies of Bridgerton or the poignant narrative of Anne With an E, he finds joy in the rich storytelling and historical settings of these shows.

⠀

A computer programmer by profession, he has a strong grasp of the intricacies of code and the ability to solve problems analytically. This dual identity defines his diverse skill set, merging the worlds of creative expression and technology. From fine-tuning code to crafting compelling narratives, he seamlessly navigates the intersection of technology and culture, creating a unique and dynamic tapestry that reflects his multifaceted interests and expertise.


Education Background:

Primary Education - St. John Bosco School (Grade 1), Academia De Covina (Grade 2 - 6)

Secondary Education - Academia De Covina (Grade 7 - 10), Theresian School of Cavite (Senior High School)

Tertiary Education - Cavite State University - Imus Campus with a degree of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
